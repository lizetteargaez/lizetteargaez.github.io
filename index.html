NÓS

MAGAZINE

NÓS is a risograph-printed bi-annual magazine that explores femininity as expression, resistance, rejection and reconciliation in people with different bodies.
It seeks to be a space for the expression of diversity that contributes to breaking down gender binarism. Under this disruptive quality, anti-design was chosen for the brand identity. Likewise, under the concept of street and community, inspiration was sought within the urban art style: graffiti.
